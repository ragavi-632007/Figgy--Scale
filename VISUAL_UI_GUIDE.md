## 2. Main Dashboard (MainWrapper)

Once activated, the app provides a real-time command center for gig workers, featuring live demand maps and parametric insurance status.

### 2.1 Home — Live Demand Heatmap
The home screen shows active hotspots (e.g., T Nagar, Chennai), live order density, and recent delivery activity on a high-fidelity map.

![Home Dashboard](/c:/Users/sridh/Downloads/Figgybackend-main%20(2)/Figgybackend-main/screenshots/23_home_demand_1774778187806.png)

### 2.2 Earnings — Income Analytics
Detailed breakdown of weekly earnings, active hours, and trips with AI-driven insights to maximize hourly rates.

![Earnings Screen](/c:/Users/sridh/Downloads/Figgybackend-main%20(2)/Figgybackend-main/screenshots/24_earnings_analytics_1774778194063.png)

### 2.3 Radar — Atmospheric Intelligence
The radar screen monitors rain, heat, and pollution levels in the worker's zone to trigger automatic parametric payouts.

![Radar Screen](/c:/Users/sridh/Downloads/Figgybackend-main%20(2)/Figgybackend-main/screenshots/25_radar_weather_1774778205820.png)

### 2.4 Profile — Worker Command Center
Quick access to daily performance metrics, bank details, and the full history of completed deliveries.
